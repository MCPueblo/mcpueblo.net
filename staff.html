Staff

The list of staff members in MCPueblo.

While I'm still a student at a vocational school, I am working my way towards becoming a computer technician. Other than Minecraft, I also play a wide variety of Nintendo games from every kind of console, with my favorite games being Legend of Zelda and Smash Bros (I main Pichu and Wolf).

In the past, I've played other games such as NHL and Fortnite -- however, I always seem to return to my gaming roots of playing Minecraft.
