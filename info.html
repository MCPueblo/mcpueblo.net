What is MCPueblo?

MCPueblo is a whitelisted 1.14.4 Minecraft server with a small dedicated community, that was released on the 17th of May of 2019. MCPueblo translated means "Minecraft Village". We pride ourselves on a vanilla experience, with only a few in-game tweaks to improve the game experience, such as one player sleep, online map, custom recipes, and more. Our server is community-focused, with monthly votes on new suggestions, and community builds such as spawn. We have zero ranks that affect the gameplay at all.

What are our features?

Listed here are some of our best features that MCPueblo has to offer:

  1. Custom recipes - These include the ability to turn 2 slabs into one, a recipe for red sand that makes use of netherrack, and re-dyeable items/blocks. You can find the full list of recipes here.
  2. Elytra drop - When you kill the ender dragon, an elytra is always dropped. This provides two alternative ways of obtaining elytras, whenever you prefer exploring the end ships or killing the ender dragon. Shulkers will also drop 2 shulker shells instead of one.
  3. One player sleep - Only one member is required to sleep in the server, so there's no need to ask all the other members to sleep.
  4. Online Map - Also known as Dynmap, this helpful map lets you see where players or bases are located, or where to live in the world.
  5. Creative world - This world is made for the purpose of testing builds and redstone prototypes. It is a superflat world with creative mode. To get to the world simply type /creative, and to get back exactly where you came from type /back in the creative world.
  6. /portalcalc - This command can calculate where to place your nether portals in both the overworld and the nether using coordinates. It should be always used when making portals in the nether to avoid conflict with other portals.
  7. DiscordSRV - This feature lets you chat to or from MCPueblo's Discord server, so you never miss out on anything that may happen only in-game.
  8. /home & /spawn - These commands are here for your convenience. You can have up to 3 homes.
  9. Instant leaf decay - When all logs inside a tree has been chopped down, all the surrounding leaves will break instantly, meaning no more waiting for it to decay.
  10. Building Challenges - MCPueblo does weekly building challenges of a wide variety of themes and topics. One must post in the corresponding post their entry, and the winner will get an extra home and a small prize in-game prize sent to them.

What are our rules?

These are the server rules which you have to follow, else you may get banned. All rules have a 3-strike system, meaning that if you break any rule 3 times, you will be banned from MCPueblo. We reserve the right to unwhitelist or ban anybody who doesn't meet our standards for a MCPueblo member.

  1. Be mature - Maturity is key to become a good member of MCPueblo. Please do not troll people, don't talk about sensitive topics like politics or drugs, and avoid excessive swearing. Inmature arguments, attitudes or behavior will not be tolerated at all.
  2. No spamming or advertising - Advertising another server than isn't MCPueblo will get you banned. Don't spam colored chat or repetitive content, as we want a clean chat.
  3. Stay active - We ask members to remain active in MCPueblo at least a few times per week. If it has been a week since the last time you've been online in the server, you will be contacted to explain your inactivity. If there's no reply after another week, you will be kicked and unwhitelisted from the server. Members can rejoin at any time with the Discord link below if they wish to return.
  4. Do not grief or steal - Nobody should have their home ruined, just somebody blew your house up and stole your belongings. Don't grief or steal anything, not even as a joke. Griefing and stealing is pointless, as we have plugins to track every item taken and every block broken/placed by any player.
  5. No cheating or hacking - We do not permit the use of mods and clients designed for cheating, such as X-Ray, aimbot, unauthorized flying, etc. Basic mods such as Optifine, minimaps, inventory organizers are allowed. In case you're not sure if the mod is permitted, ask a staff member for information.
  6. Respect nature - We like clean things, so why should you trash the natural environment? Don't leave the canopy of trees up, don't do a poor job of flattening land, don't leave one block towers, and repair creeper holes if you get blown up by one.

How do I join MCPueblo?

Do you want to join MCPueblo? Then whitelist yourself using this application form. After that you must join the Discord with this invite, for a quick response if you are accepted in the server.

Once you're accepted, you may join the server at play.mcpueblo.net on version 1.14.4.

Do we have a Discord link?

It is expected that you join the Discord group with this invite, to keep in touch with other members and the server. It is also the way you can see in-game chat at any time without joining the server.
